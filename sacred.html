Description:
Produced by Anurag Kashyap and Motwane under Phantom Productions.
"Scared Game" is a gripping Indian crime thriller series that follows Sartaj Singh trouble Mumbai police officer who receives a chilling phone call from the notorious gangster Ganesh Gaitonde.

Gaitonde warns Sartaj to save the city within 25 days, setting of a chain of events that unrabels a complex web of crying, corporation and political conspirancy.
