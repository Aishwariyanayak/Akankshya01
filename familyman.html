Produced by Anurag Kashyap and Motwane under Phantom Productions.
"The family Man" is an Indian action-drama series that follows the story of Srikant Tiwari, a middle-class man working as a senior officer in the National Investigation Agency (NIA), who must balance his high-pressure, secretive job of protecting the country from terrorism while also trying to maintain a normal family life with his wife and children, often struggling with the impact his dangerous work has on his loved ones
