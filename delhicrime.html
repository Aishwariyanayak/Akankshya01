Description:
Produced by Golden Karaven under lvanhoe Films Productions.
"Delhi Crime" is a Netflix web series based on the real-life 2012 Delhi gang rape case, focusing on the investigation led by Deputy Commissioner of Police (DCP) Vartika Chaturvedi as she attempts to apprehend the perpetrators of the brutal assault, while navigating intense public outrage and the complex social dynamics of Delhi in the aftermath of the crime.
